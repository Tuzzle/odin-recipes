Spaghetti Squash Chicken

This is the Spaghetti Squash Chicken Parm that my ex-girlfriend used to make all the time!

She gave me the recipe before I moved to another city beacuse I loved it that much. It really is a great dish to show off to friends or family or a date. But it is still easy to make and not that long of a process once you do it once or twice!

Ingredients

  • Olive Oil
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Spaghetti Squash
  • two boneless chicken breasts
  • Eggs, whisked
  • Italian seasoned breadcrumbs
  • Preferred Pasta Sauce
  • Grated parmesan
  • Shredded mozzarella
  • Basil
  • Red Pepper Flakes optional

Steps

  1. Preheat Oven to 400 degrees
  2. Cut spaghetti squash in half vertically and scrape out the seeds with a spoon
  3. Place on a baking sheet with olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  4. Flip them over and poke multiple holes in the skin with a fork
  5. Pound the chicken breast until they are about 1 inch thick.
  6. Dip the chicken in the whisked egg.
  7. Dip the chicken in Italian seasoned breadcrumbs
  8. Bake them on a cooling rack that has been placed on a baking sheet on the middle rack of the oven
  9. Place the squash on the rack right aboe it.
  10. Take the squash out after 40 minutes, chicken out after 50.
  11. While you are waiting for the chicken to finish, take th squash out and scrape it with a fork to form spaghetti.
  12. Add pasta sauce to the spaghetti inside the squash and grated parmesan.
  13. Mix it all together.
  14. Take the chicken out and slice it.
  15. Put the squash back on the baking sheet and add the chicken.
  16. Add extra sauce and mozzarella cheese.
  17. Broil on low until the cheese is melted.
  18. Top with basil and red pepper flakes if desired
