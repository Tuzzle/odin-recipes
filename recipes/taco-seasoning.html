Taco Seasoning

This is my mom's favorite taco seasoning!

She would always make this with ground beef, but I have used it on chicken as well!

Ingredients

  • 1/4th tsp garlic powder
  • 1/4th tsp onion powder
  • 1/4th tsp oregano
  • 1/2 tsp parika
  • 1-1/2 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp salt

Steps

  1. Mix together and keep it in an airtight glass jar
